Project Description

The Fremont Collective is a re-purposed former warehouse in Seattle that Heliotrope helped convert into retail and restaurant space for the developer Evolution Projects, and home to three Heliotrope tenant improvement projects: the mountain sports retailer evo, and two nationally recognized restaurants, Joule and The Whale Wins. Scope of work included interior and exterior architecture, finishes, landscaping and lighting.
